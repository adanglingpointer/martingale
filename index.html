Martingale Calculator

The Martingale is a method of betting that relies on a very simple premis: every time you lose, you double down.

In the world of infinite money, this means that one could reliably depend on winning as long as they double down on their loss each time until they eventually win.

Unfortunately, money is limited, and casinos put betting limits on games. However, this doesn't mean that a reliable Martingale method can not still be applied. How? By betting in a favorable position, every time.

In the case of roulette, one can simply wait for a bet to come out thrice before betting on the opposite. For example, if black hits three times in a row, then we can bet the minimum bet on red for the fourth spin. This provides us the statistical benefit of the Martingale method while risking a fraction of the wager.
