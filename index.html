The Martingale is a strategy of betting that relies on a very simple premise: every time you lose, you double down on the next round.

Theoretically, if you have infinite money to wager, you will be guaranteed to win on the nth bet by doubling down each time.

Unfortunately, money is limited, and casinos put betting limits on games. However, you can slightly increase your odds by betting on a favorable position from the beginning.

In this scenario for a game of Roulette, it means waiting for an outer position (odd/even, high/low, or black/red) to hit x amount of times in a row before the first bet is placed.
